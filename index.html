About

Run for your life mini marathon is charity event. We promote fitness programs for all ages. This marathon will donate all the profits to increasing youth physicial fitness programs in schools in Jacksonville Fl.

Join The Journey and The Fun.

People that participate in the event have the option of getting a discount to outr next event. Bring your friends and family for the event. Everyone is invited to the small after party.
